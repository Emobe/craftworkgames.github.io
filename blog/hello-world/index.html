More will be posted here in the future in terms of the development of MonoGame.Extended. For now, there is a couple things worth mentioning.

This leads to the maintenance sprint; I have scheduled a sprint for the repository for the month of June. Hopefully I can make some progress on the existing issues and get a good feel for what is the current state of MonoGame.Extended.

Stay tuned for more development details and future plans.
